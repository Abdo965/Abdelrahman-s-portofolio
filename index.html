My Teaching Philosophy

I believe in making learning an adventure. My approach centers on creating engaging, hands-on experiences that connect with students emotionally and intellectually. By fostering curiosity and making complex topics accessible and fun, I aim to not only teach coding and AI but to inspire problem-solvers and innovators. High student satisfaction and their desire to continue learning are my greatest rewards.

Ages 6-8: Playful Foundations

Introducing concepts through visual tools like WeDo and Scratch-like platforms (App Inventor basics), focusing on storytelling, basic logic, and the joy of making things work.

Ages 9-13: Building & Creating

Transitioning to text-based coding with Python, building simple games, interactive apps with App Inventor, and exploring basic robotics with Arduino and IoT concepts with ESP.

Ages 14-18: Advanced Exploration & AI

Deeper dives into Python for AI, machine learning concepts, advanced App Inventor projects, and more complex IoT solutions. Focus on project development and real-world applications.
